In the upcoming electricity dominant world, fossil fuel won’t be shining as like now.

Despite those renewable sources like solar, water,etc.., A non renewable source will play a major role in electricity generation.

That source is “NATURAL GAS”.

As per “ International Energy Agency -2017” natural gas was the second leading contributor towards the generation of electricity upto 23% of the total world generation than any other renewable and non- renewable sources. So, this conference is to spread the awareness about the generation of electricity through “ THE NATRURAL GAS”.
